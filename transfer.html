Transfer

New Signings

When it comes to our 2022 season, we were very careful with the talent and players we would bring into our roster. We only made three transfers into the team in this year's summer and winter window. We bought in amazing talent along with promising talent to build for the future. First off we acquired Antonio Rudiger from Chelsea for a free transfer. The centerback will provide additional support in our defense. His contract will last until 2026. As for our second transfer we acquired midfielder Aurlien Tchouameni for a total of 80 million U.S dollars from AS Monaco. His contract will last until 2028. And lastly in December we were able to win the bidding war and acquire 16 year old brazilian wonderboy Endrick for a total of 60 million U.S dollars. In conclusion, this transfer window has shown us that the team is in excellent hands for the future as we are developing amazing talent that will build the team in the long run.
